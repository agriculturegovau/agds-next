Our shadow tokens enable us to use drop shadows consistently and systematically across the Export Service.

We have three drop shadow tokens:

- **sm**: For clickable cards
- **md**: For popovers
- **lg**: For modals

```jsx live
<Flex
	gap={1.5}
	css={{
		width: '100%',
	}}
>
	<Flex
		flexGrow={1}
		paddingX={1}
		paddingY={2}
		rounded
		css={{ boxShadow: tokens.shadow.sm }}
	>
		sm
	</Flex>
	<Flex
		flexGrow={1}
		paddingX={1}
		paddingY={2}
		rounded
		css={{ boxShadow: tokens.shadow.md }}
	>
		md
	</Flex>
	<Flex
		flexGrow={1}
		paddingX={1}
		paddingY={2}
		rounded
		css={{ boxShadow: tokens.shadow.lg }}
	>
		lg
	</Flex>
</Flex>
```
