## Length and style

1-2 words for each stage. Limit characters

Write in Sentence case.

## Language

Use plain language.

Avoid punctuation such as full stops, commas, or semicolons.

Avoid articles such as the, an, or a

Refer to the glossary for general information.

## Voice and tone

Refer to the [TFTM Voice and tone guide](/content/voice-and-tone) for guidance.
